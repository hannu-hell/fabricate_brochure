SOME OF OUR WORK

The Mechanical Flower

The Mechanical Flower entirely designed using CAD software is a perfect piece of decoration. This serves as a perfect example of an innovative mechanical design combined with precise motion. It would definitely be the center of attention at any occasion with its mesmerizing shades of lights and movement patterns. The flower is powered using Solar Panels and is equipped with a LIPO (Lithium Polymer) Battery which can be recharged with the solar panels attached to the leaves. The stem of the flower is able to move and so are the petals. It is fully 3D printed and all part can be replaced easily if damaged. We welcome your ideas and share with us your concepts and upon discussion we can bring them to life.

The Motion Sensitive Signage Board

The Signage board lights up with the colors of the logo and is animated with precise stepping of the lights in intervals. The lights are able to display the required color patterns. The Motion Sensor embedded in the board triggers the light animation pattern. This could be adapted to many kinds of sensors such as Photoresistor Cells which trigger the animation depending on the ambient lighting of the environment. Clients can consult us to present their idea and we will try to make it a reality.
